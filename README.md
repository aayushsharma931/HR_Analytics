# HR Analytics using Python

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee data from the IBM HR Analytics dataset using Python. The objective is to explore workforce trends, employee attrition, salary distribution, job satisfaction, and department-wise insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

The project demonstrates essential data analysis techniques such as data loading, data quality checks, visualization, and business insight generation.

---

## Dataset

* **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance
* **Records:** 1,470 employees
* **Features:** 35

---

## Project Workflow

1. Load the dataset
2. Explore the dataset

6. Answer business questions

---

## Key Insights

* The overall employee attrition rate is approximately **16.12%**.
* Research & Development has the largest workforce.
* Sales employees have the highest average monthly income among the departments.
* Employees who work overtime tend to experience higher attrition.
* Employees who leave the company generally have shorter tenure than those who stay.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Monitor overtime to reduce employee burnout.
* Improve retention strategies for employees during their first few years.
* Review compensation for lower-paid job roles.
* Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
* Continue investing in employee development and career growth.
